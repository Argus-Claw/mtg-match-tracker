◇ MTG Match Tracker ◇

Life tracking, multiplayer sync, game history & stats — all in your browser.

Quick Start

  1. Open the app → Log in or Register (email + password)
  2. Tap New Game
  3. Pick your format → Add players → Start Game
  4. Tap the life total area to adjust life — right/top = +1, left/bottom = −1
  5. When done → End Game → pick winner → Save

Starting a New Game

  1. Pick a format — this sets starting life and enables/disables commander damage tracking
  2. Add players — tap "Add Player" for each person. You can:
    • Add yourself (logged-in user)
    • Add friends (if you've connected via the Friends page)
    • Add guests by name
  3. Drag to reorder players in the setup screen using the ☰ handle
  4. For Commander/Brawl: optionally enter Commander name and Commander colors
  5. Tap Start Game
If your app closes mid-game, don't worry — the game auto-saves to your browser. When you come back and tap New Game, you'll see a "Resume Game" option.

Life Tracking

Tap Zones

Each player card has invisible tap zones for adjusting life:

+ top
− left     + right
− bottom

  • Tap the right side or top of the life area → +1 life
  • Tap the left side or bottom → −1 life
  • Faint +/− signs on the card show you which zone is which
  • A colored flash (green = gain, red = loss) confirms the change
  • Rapid taps accumulate — a floating +3 or −5 shows total change
Tap zones are rotation-aware — if you rotate a card, the zones rotate with it so +/− always makes sense from your viewing angle.

Player Cards

Card Features

  • Mana color dots — tap a color circle (W/U/B/R/G) to change the card's color theme
  • Player name — tap to edit name, deck name, and commander (dashed underline = editable)
  • Minimize (−) — collapses a card to a compact bar showing just name + life
  • Remove (✕) — removes a player (only if 3+ players)
  • Death overlay — skull appears when life ≤ 0, poison ≥ 10, or commander damage ≥ 21

Counters

Tap the Counters button at the bottom of a player card to expand:

  • 💀 Poison — 10 = lethal
  • ⚡ Energy
  • ⭐ Experience

Commander Damage (Commander/Brawl only)

Tap Cmd Dmg to expand commander damage tracking:

  • Shows damage taken from each opponent
  • Tap + to add damage (also subtracts 1 life automatically)
  • Tap + Cmdr to add a partner commander tracker for an opponent
  • Tap a commander label to rename it
  • 21 damage from a single commander = 💀 lethal

Drag & Rotate

Below each player card are two buttons:

  • ↻ Rotate — cycles the card through 0° → 90° → 180° → 270°. Useful for players sitting across from you — flip their card upside-down so they can read it.
  • ☰ Drag — press and drag to rearrange player positions on the grid. Swap any two players.

Turn & Storm Counters

In the header between the format title and toolbar:

  • TURN — tap +/− to track turns. Advancing the turn resets storm count to 0.
  • STORM — tap +/− to count spells cast this turn.

Tools Panel

Tap 🎲 Tools in the toolbar to open:

  • Dice Roller — select die type (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20) and tap Roll. Animated result.
  • Coin Flip — Heads or Tails with animation.
  • Who Goes First? — randomly picks a player from the game.
  • Storm & Turn counters — duplicate controls (also in the header).

Multiplayer (Multi-Device Sync)

Let each player control their own life total from their own phone.

Host (you)

  1. Start a game normally
  2. Tap Share in the toolbar
  3. A QR code and 6-character code appear
  4. Other players scan the QR or open the link
  5. Your screen is the main display — shows all players, synced in real time

Guests (other players)

  1. Scan the QR code or open the shared link
  2. Tap your name to claim your player slot
  3. Tap Join Game
  4. You'll see your card full-size with other players in a compact list below
  5. Tap your card to adjust your life — changes sync to the host screen instantly

Connection Status

  • ● Shared = connected and syncing
  • ● Reconnecting... = connection dropped, auto-recovering

The host sends a sync heartbeat every 5 seconds. If a guest's phone goes to sleep or loses connection, it will automatically reconnect and catch up when the screen wakes.

If someone accidentally closes their browser, they can re-open the same link and re-tap their name to rejoin. Their life total is preserved on the host.
Multiplayer requires an account (the host must be logged in). Guests can join anonymously.

Ending & Saving a Game

End Game (Save)

  1. Tap End Game
  2. Tap the winning player(s) — crown icon appears
  3. Optionally add notes ("Turn 7 combo win", etc.)
  4. Tap Save & End Game

After saving, you get three options:

  • 🔄 Run It Back — same players, same format, reset life & counters. Instant rematch.
  • 🎲 New Game — back to format/player selection
  • Back to Menu — return to dashboard

Abandon Game

Tap Abandon to discard the game without saving. Asks for confirmation.

Game History & Stats

From the main menu:

  • Games — browse all saved games with format, date, players, winner, and life totals
  • Stats — win rates, format breakdown, and performance charts
  • Friends — add friends by searching for their username. Friends appear as player options in New Game.

FAQ

Do I need an account?

To host a game and save history, yes (free, just email + password). To join someone else's game as a guest, no — anonymous access works.

Does it work offline?

Yes — it's a PWA. The life tracker works without internet. Multiplayer sync and game saving require a connection.

Can I use it on a tablet as a table centerpiece?

Absolutely. Use Dock + 3col + Fullscreen for the best experience. Rotate cards so each player can read their own.

What happens if my phone disconnects during multiplayer?

The host keeps all state. Your phone will auto-reconnect when the screen wakes. Any changes you made while disconnected will sync back to the host.

Can two people control the same player?

Yes — multiple devices can claim the same slot. Useful if someone wants to hand off to another device.
